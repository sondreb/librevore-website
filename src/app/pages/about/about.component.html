About Librevore Village

Our story, vision, and mission

Our Story

Librevore Village began as a vision for a different way of living. In a world that often feels disconnected and focused on consumerism, we yearned for a return to community, to nature, and to the values of freedom and voluntary cooperation.

Our project was born when a small group of families decided to look for land in Montenegro where we could build a community founded on the principles of voluntaryism and healthy, sustainable living. We chose the beautiful region near Bar for its natural beauty, favorable climate for growing food, and the opportunity to create something truly special.

Today, we're in the process of establishing our village and inviting like-minded families to join us in this journey of creating a nurturing environment where our children can grow up connected to nature and supported by a community of shared values.

Our Vision

To create a thriving, self-sustained community where families can live in harmony with nature, practice voluntaryism in daily life, and raise children in an environment that nurtures their natural curiosity and freedom.

Our Mission

To establish and maintain a family village that demonstrates the viability of voluntary cooperation, healthy living practices, and sustainable food production, serving as an inspiration and blueprint for similar communities worldwide.

Location

Situated in the picturesque outskirts of Bar, Montenegro, the property we are in negoations to buy offers the perfect balance of natural beauty with accessibility to city amenities when needed.

Scouting for land

One family has already relocated to Bar, Montenegro and have spent more than a year scouting the the perfect location. After a lot of effort, the perfect paradise was discovered.

The Project

The land we plan to purchase is more than 98,000 sqm and is located in a green and lush valley, surrounded by mountains and forests. There are no highways or busy roads nearby, and it's a undeveloped area away from the more tourist locations at the coast of Montenegro. We plan to accommodate approximately 5-7 families, with individual homes, shared community spaces, and extensive gardens.

Sustainability

Our community is designed with sustainability in mind, featuring organic gardens, renewable energy solutions, natural building methods, and water conservation systems.
