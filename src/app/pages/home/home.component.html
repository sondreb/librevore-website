A New Way of Living

Librevore Village is more than just homes - it's a vision for harmonious community living

Nestled in the beautiful landscape outside Bar, Montenegro, Librevore Village is a community living project focused on creating a nurturing environment for families who share values of voluntaryism, freedom, and sustainable living.

Our vision is to establish a family village where children can grow up connected to nature, where families support each other, and where we collectively work toward greater self-sufficiency through growing our own food and living more harmoniously with our surroundings.
