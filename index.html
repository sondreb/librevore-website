Strong community living

Librevore is a concept of community living of families and friends, joined together under the ethical philosophies of voluntaryism and healthy living.

Joining together in a physical location, building homes together and sharing the passions for healthy living, both physically and mentally. Increasing the spiritial connection with Earth and all living creatures.

Features

  • Shared Ethical Foundations
  • Community Garden Cultivation.
  • Family Friendly Housing.
  • Open and Friendly Atmosphere.

Benefits

  • Healthy Living
  • Community Connection
  • Self-Reliance
  • Sustainble Lifestyle

Core Principles

  • Voluntaryism

    All human interactions should be voluntary, free of threats of violence and violence.

  • Earth

    Living of the land and in harmony with plants and animals, not inflicting unnecessary pain and suffering.

  • Families

    Coming together as families, working together and collaborate on home schooling and conscious parenting.

Project Status

The project is currently in the process of finding suiteable land in Montenegro.

  • ~5-7 Families
  • 5-10 Hectares Plot

After the initial plot purchase, the construction of homes will start immediately and community buildings and gardens will follow. There will be a shared green house, shared spa, shared outdoors barbeque and dining area and more.
